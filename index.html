About Me

Hi, I’m a passionate fitness coach and endurance athlete dedicated to helping people build strength, confidence, and lifelong fitness habits. I am an IIT graduate and a software engineer, and my own journey from the corporate world to endurance sports has shaped the way I approach fitness today. I have completed an Ironman triathlon and multiple marathons, experiences that have taught me the power of discipline, consistency, and mental strength.


My approach focuses on sustainable training, proper technique, and gradual progress so that anyone—regardless of their starting point—can become stronger and healthier. Through structured workouts, endurance training, and functional fitness, I aim to create a supportive community where people enjoy the process of improving their fitness.
